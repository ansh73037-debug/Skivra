FAQ

Questions answered

Is Skivra really free to use? +
Yes. The core Skivra experience — creating a profile, listing skills, getting matched, and exchanging knowledge via chat and video — is completely free. We offer optional Pro and Team plans with advanced analytics, priority matching, and premium session tools.
What if my skills aren't technical? +
All skills are welcome. Language coaching, music, cooking, photography, public speaking, yoga instruction, business strategy — if you know it, someone wants to learn it. Some of our most popular exchanges happen between creative and technical skill sets.
How does skill matching work? +
Our matching engine looks at skill complementarity (you teach X, they want X; they teach Y, you want Y), availability overlap, location or timezone proximity, communication style preferences, and past ratings. The more sessions you complete, the more accurate your matches become.
What if a session doesn't go well? +
We take quality seriously. You can leave an honest review after every session and report any issues to our Trust & Safety team. Accounts with consistently poor reviews are removed from the platform. Your experience and safety matter to us.
Can I use Skivra for professional development? +
Absolutely. Many companies and freelancers use Skivra Teams to upskill their workforce through internal skill exchanges and cross-functional peer learning. It's collaborative professional development without the training budget.
How do I know someone is who they say they are? +
Skivra verifies users via email, LinkedIn, and optionally government ID. Verified profiles display a blue badge. We also verify skills through community endorsements and completed session history, so you can trust the ratings you see.

The Process

From signup to your
first session in 60 minutes.

Skivra removes every barrier between you and your next learning experience. Here's exactly how it works.

1

Create your profile

Sign up with email or continue with Google or LinkedIn. Build your public profile with a photo, a short bio, and links to your portfolio or work. This is your Skivra identity — it's how potential exchange partners decide to reach out.

⏱ Takes about 5 minutes
2

Add what you know and what you want to learn

List your skills with a proficiency level (beginner, intermediate, expert) and how many hours per week you can commit. Then add the skills you want to develop. Be specific — "React hooks and context API" matches better than just "React."

💡 More detail = better matches
3

Review your match suggestions

Within minutes, your dashboard will surface your top matches — people who teach what you want and want to learn what you teach. Each card shows their profile, skills, rating, and timezone so you can find the right fit at a glance.

🎯 Average: 8 quality matches within 24 hours
4

Send an exchange request

Tap "Request Exchange" on any match, write a brief personal note about what you'd like to learn and offer, and hit send. Skivra notifies them instantly. Most requests get a response within a few hours.

✉ Personalized requests get 3× more responses
5

Schedule and start your first session

Once both sides agree, pick a time using the built-in scheduler. Skivra handles timezone conversion automatically. At the scheduled time, click "Join Session" — no downloads needed. Your first exchange is live.

🎥 HD video, screen share, and whiteboard included

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2025 · Effective: June 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you create a Skivra account, we collect information you provide directly: your name, email address, profile photo, skills, bio, and any portfolio links you add. When you use our platform, we also collect usage data including session metadata (duration, frequency), messages (encrypted at rest), video session logs, and device and browser information.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not show you ads. We do not share your data with advertisers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and improve the Skivra service, including:

  • Matching you with compatible skill exchange partners
  • Powering AI recommendations based on your skills and goals
  • Sending transactional emails (session reminders, match notifications)
  • Detecting and preventing fraud and abuse
  • Improving our matching algorithm and platform features
  • Complying with legal obligations

3. Data Sharing

Your public profile information (name, photo, skills, bio, ratings) is visible to other Skivra members. Your private messages and personal details (email address, phone number if provided) are never shared with other users.

We share data with service providers who help us operate Skivra (cloud infrastructure, video streaming, email delivery) under strict data processing agreements. We may share data if required by law or to protect the safety of our users.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we remove your personal information within 30 days, except where we are required by law to retain it for longer. Aggregated, anonymized usage data may be retained indefinitely for research and product improvement purposes.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data at any time from your account settings. If you are located in the European Union, you have additional rights under GDPR, including the right to data portability and the right to object to processing. Contact us at privacy@skivra.com to exercise any of these rights.

6. Cookies

We use strictly necessary cookies to keep you logged in and protect your session. We use analytics cookies (which you can opt out of) to understand how people use Skivra so we can improve the product. We do not use advertising cookies.

7. Security

We take security seriously. Messages are encrypted at rest and in transit. We use industry-standard authentication practices and conduct regular security audits. In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you within 72 hours.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@skivra.com or by post at Skivra, Inc., 100 Montgomery Street, San Francisco, CA 94104.
